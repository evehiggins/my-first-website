Reflection

The first lab focused on learning how to navigate Visual Studio Code, push updates to GitHub, and begin thinking about the overall direction and structure of our final website. While working through this lab, I realized that I may struggle with keeping my code organized in a way that makes it easy to locate and update specific elements later on. That said, this experience was encouraging, as HTML felt more intuitive to me from the beginning compared to languages like Python and R. Overall, the lab helped me feel more confident starting the technical side of the project while also clarifying areas where I know I will need to be more deliberate as the site grows.
